A simple interactive chat bot using Nodejs and Express

2B a versatile Discord bot with a range of interactive and utility features such as greetings , entertainment(gifs and mini games), and information retrieval (bitcoin currency status, cricket score). The bot also includes utility commands such for generating random numbers and creating personalized message banners. Leveraged Discord.js, JavaScript, and Node.js to enhance community engagement and provide real-time information and interactive experiences for users
